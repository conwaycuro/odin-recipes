Guacamole

Guacamole is a beloved Mexican classic that combines the creamy richness of ripe avocados with the brightness of lime, the bite of onion, and the freshness of tomato and cilantro. Traditionally made with just a few wholesome ingredients, guacamole is both incredibly simple and endlessly customizable. Its vibrant flavor and satisfying texture make it a perfect appetizer, topping, or side dish. Whether you're serving it with crispy tortilla chips, spooning it over tacos, or adding a dollop to your favorite bowl, guacamole brings a burst of freshness and a hint of zest to any meal. Best of all, it's quick to prepare and made entirely from natural, nutrient-rich ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1–2 tablespoons fresh lime juice (to taste)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1–2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 small jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional, for heat)

Recipe

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork or potato masher until you reach your desired consistency.
  3. Add the diced tomato, red onion, garlic, lime juice, cilantro (if using), and jalapeño (if using).
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add more lime juice if desired.
  5. Serve immediately, or store with plastic wrap pressed directly on the surface to minimize browning.
